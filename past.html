The Past

I was born the second child into an eventual family of 6, in Orem, Utah on August 28th, 1982. As the oldest boy I asuumed the role of the rebel. Art, music, and skateboarding took up most of my attention. I first ventured into the world of coding when I started collecting hard to find punk music. I built a basic tape trading website, met some kids, moved to Milwuakee, and formed some bands. 15 years later, back in Utah, a group of friends including my girlfriend Marisa, discovered their own interest in coding and convinced me to come along to a meetup at Devpoint Labs. I was surprised that I remembered some of the basic code and concepts and was once again excited about the challange of coding.
